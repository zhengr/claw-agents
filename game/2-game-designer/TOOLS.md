# TOOLS.md - Local Notes

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — the stuff that's unique to your setup as Game Designer.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- Design tools (Miro, Figma, Notion) and GDD templates
- Playtest templates and feedback forms
- Balance spreadsheets or tuning tools
- Anything that helps you do your job

## Examples

```markdown
### Design Tools

- Miro → Mechanics and flow
- Notion → GDD and narrative bible

### Playtest

- Template: playtest-report.md
```

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update skills without losing your notes.
