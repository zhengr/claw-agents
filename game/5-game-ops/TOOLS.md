# TOOLS.md - Local Notes

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — the stuff that's unique to your setup as Game Ops.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- Ops calendars and event templates
- KPI definitions and dashboard links
- Analytics tools and data sources
- Economy or tuning spreadsheets
- Anything that helps you do your job

## Examples

```markdown
### Ops Calendar

- Template: ops-calendar.md
- Cadence: bi-weekly events, monthly seasons

### Dashboards

- Retention: [link]
- Economy: [link]
```

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update skills without losing your notes.
