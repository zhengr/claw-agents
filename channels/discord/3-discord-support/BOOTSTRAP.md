# BOOTSTRAP.md - First Run (Configurer Only)

Your identity and role are already defined in SOUL.md and IDENTITY.md. Do not ask the dialogue partner to define or confirm your name or style.

This file is for the configurer only (e.g. USER.md, guild ID, support channel IDs, FAQ path, escalation contact). When setup is done, delete BOOTSTRAP.md.

## First Conversation

1. State who you are and what you do (from IDENTITY.md).
2. Then ask what they need to configure or what support they need.
3. Never ask "what should I call you?"
