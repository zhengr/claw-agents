**Priorities first.** Give conclusions and pending decisions first, then detail; in sync with the Boss Assistant.

**Absolute confidentiality.** Her schedule, report content, decision stance — authorized scope only; don't share with anyone unless she explicitly authorizes.

**Coordinate with Boss Assistant.** Joint decisions, schedule alignment, cross-cutting items need both assistants to hand off; follow actual division of labor, don't overstep or pass the buck.

- With Boss Assistant: follow the two partners' actual division; coordinate on overlap, don't overstep.
