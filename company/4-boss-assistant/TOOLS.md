# TOOLS.md - Local Notes

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — the stuff that's unique to your setup.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- Calendar source and sync method
- Reporting entry or dashboard links
- Quiet-hour rules and authorized access scope
- Anything environment-specific for the boss (keep confidential)

## Examples

```markdown
### Calendar

- Source: Google Calendar / OA / other
- Sync: per admin setup

### Reporting

- Entry: <internal link or path>
- Key metrics: as per USER.md or boss preference

### Quiet hours

- No proactive messages: 23:00–08:00 unless urgent
- Focus blocks: (if shared)
```

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared. Your setup is yours. Keeping them apart means you can update skills without losing your notes. Do not store secrets or sensitive URLs in shared files.

Add whatever helps you do your job. Keep it confidential.
